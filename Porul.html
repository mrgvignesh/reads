Our political philosophy is to build a competent state, defined by four pillars inspired by the timeless wisdom of the Thirukkural's verse 385.

குறள் : இயற்றலும் ஈட்டலுங் காத்தலும் காத்த

                 வகுத்தலும் வல்ல தரசு

Translated as: A state is competent that can create (wealth), accumulate it, guard it, and wisely distribute what is guarded.

The Four Pillars:

  • Creation of wealth: A state must be able to generate resources and opportunities.
  • Accumulation: It must effectively manage and grow its wealth.
  • Protection: The state needs to safeguard its assets and people from threats.
  • Wise distribution: It must equitably and intelligently allocate resources for the well-being of all citizens.

We are here for ...

  1. Fight for good civic amenities of citizens. We're here to fight for immediate infrastructure and civic concerns that impact daily life, ensuring effective and transparent governance at the grassroots level.
  2. Inclusivity for all people from all walks of life without any bias to any gender. We're here to Champion equality and representation, creating a society where every voice is valued and protected regardless of identity.
  3. Drive community growth by supporting initiatives that promote grass root innovation. We're here to invest in local, small-scale projects and technologies that are born from and directly benefit the community.
  4. Promote Healthy lifestyle and educating the effects of western lifestyle and quick foods. We're here to advocate for well-being through public health campaigns, encouraging nutritious, traditional diets and active living.
  5. Promote arts and sports among youth. We're here to partner with local communities and establish programs and infrastructure to nurture creative expression, physical fitness, and team-building skills in the next generation.
  6. Encourage sustainable ways of living. We're here to promote policies and educational drives that favor environmental stewardship, waste reduction, and the conservation of natural resources.
  7. Preserve rich and varied heritage of our land. we're committed to protecting and celebrating all aspects of our cultural and natural history. This includes safeguarding ancient traditions, historical sites, diverse ecosystems, and indigenous knowledge for future generations.
  8. Promote collective prosperity. we're here to promote collective prosperity moving beyond individual gain to focus on the shared well-being of a community and ultimately our country..
  9. Promote simple and fulfilling life for all. we're here for an utopian society where art is accessible to all and social value is based on character, not wealth.. Inspired from the Aim of Auroville
  10. Change through Political Participation. we're here to bring a change in the political spectrum rising above differences and promoting change through participation.
